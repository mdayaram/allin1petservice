Random Pet Tip:

Pet Sitting

When you're traveling for business or pleasure...
Whether it's an overnight meeting, a weekend getaway or a long awaited vacation, we provide personalized in-home pet care services. In-home care allows your pet to remain in the comfort of their own environment, reducing stress for them and giving you peace of mind.

We will visit your home to feed, care for and play with your pet. We will have a responsibility for bot your pet and your home.
The advantages of our services are:

The pet remains in his or her safe, secure environment, where all the sights, smells, and sounds are familiar. Pets adjust better to their owner's absence and experience lesss anxiety and stress when they can remain in their home environment.
The pet follows his or her regular diet, excercise, and playtime routine with no interruption to upset your pet.
We will administer medication and other special care; get medical attention in the event of illness.
The pet is not exposed to illnesses or parasites from other animals.
The pet is not traumatized by travel in a car or an unfamiliar environment.
The pet receives love, proper care and personal attention while you are away.

The pet owner's home is made more secure by crime deterrent services. We will also provide vital home-care services while you are away, including bringing in the mail or newspaper, plant care and watering, placing trash outside for pickup or rotating the lights and opening or closing blinds to make your house look lived in. Your home won't look vacant to thieves casing the neighborhood.
