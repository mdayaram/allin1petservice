Behavior and Obedience Training

All In One Pet Service offers training tailored to meet your needs and the needs of your best friend. We offer positive-reinforcement private lessons, in-home training, and behavioral modification training.

Obedience training is one of the best things you can do for your dog or puppy and yourself. Obedience training is one of the most important aspects of raising a dog. In fact, a well trained dog is by far a happier dog! Training opens up a line of communication between you and your dog. Effective communication is necessary to instruct your dog about what you want them to do.

Dogs are social animals and without proper training they will behave like animals. Nearly all behavior problems are perfectly normal canine activities that occur at the wrong time or place or are directed at the wrong thing. The key to preventing or treating behavior problems is learning to teach your dog to redirect their natural behavior to outlets that are acceptable in the domestic setting. A well trained dog is more confident and can more safely be allowed a greater amount of freedom than an untrained one.

Training serves to strengthen the bond between a dog and their owner. Training can enrich your relationship and make living together more enjoyable. It builds communication, understanding, and mutual respect, and effectively demonstrates to your dog that you are the leader of the pack (commonly referred to as the �Alpha�).

Obedience training can be used to help fulfill some of your dog�s basic needs, such as exercise, the security of knowing what is expected, a feeling of accomplishment, and constructive social interaction. Training will give your dog a job to do and can be useful in redirecting some of the mental energy of an animal that was meant for work.
